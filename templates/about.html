About Us

RDP is a political party being brought to full and live existence by youths across the nation of Kenya.

We are focussed on revitalizing the unfulfilled goals of devolution that have not yet been fulfilled and are seemingly not being fulfilled soon. As RDP, we are focussed on youth empowerment and hence the major shareholders of the party. We are creating a platform where the youths, women and the marginalized can get opportunities to represent their various regions in political positions across the country
